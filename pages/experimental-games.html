Experimental Games

In February-March 2023 I developed 4 digital games as part of the 'Experimental Game Design' course in a team with 2 other students. Each game was developed in 2 weeks and explores a different theme.

Frog Choir

Theme: Game & Watch

Year: 2023

Engine: Unity

Time: 2 weeks (20h/week)

Team Size: 3

Role: Original Idea, Programmer (Solo), Designer

Frog choir is made in the style of Game & Watch. You play as a choir conductor that must keep the frog choir from going off pitch. The better you are able to keep the frogs on pitch, the larger the audience and your high score.

Comments:

Originally, I envisioned each frog would have a different voice register (soprano, alto, etc.) as well as have different singing parts. Like a real choir! We weren’t quite able to realize that idea in reality and I still wonder how the choir would sound if we did.

Made by myself with a little knowledge of HTML and CSS
